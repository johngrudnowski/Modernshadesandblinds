How We Work

Three steps. No surprises.

Every project follows the same rhythm. It starts in your home, with samples in hand. It ends with installation handled by us, not subcontractors.

01

In your home, with the samples.

[PLACEHOLDER COPY] We come to you. We bring physical samples of fabric, hardware, and motorization options — the actual textures, not images on a screen.

We measure every window, note the light at the time of day you’ll be in the room, and ask about how each space gets used. The conversation typically takes 60–90 minutes.

There is no charge for the initial visit, and no obligation to proceed.

Photo: Jake holding samples to a window
02

Design decisions, together.

[PLACEHOLDER COPY] After the in-home visit, we narrow the field. We help you choose between options based on what you actually want from each room — how much light, how much privacy, how easy to operate.

We quote a firm number for every option. No surprises later, no add-ons. Nothing is ordered until you say so.

If you’re working with a designer, we work directly with them at this stage. We handle the technical specification; they retain creative direction.

Photo: Sample selection / quote review
03

Installation, by us.

[PLACEHOLDER COPY] We install everything we sell. No subcontractors. The person who measured your windows is the person who installs.

If anything ever needs adjustment — a shade that won’t roll smoothly, a motor that needs reprogramming — there is one number to call. We stand behind every installation for as long as it’s in your home.

Photo: Installation in progress
