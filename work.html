Lake home, Minnetonka.

2024

The Specification

  • Custom-patterned valances, hand-selected fabric
  • Cellular shades for light control
  • Eleven windows, two stories
  • Collaboration with the homeowner’s designer

[PLACEHOLDER COPY] Two-story great room with eleven windows, all facing the lake. The client wanted privacy without losing the view, and a custom textile detail that would tie the room together. We worked with their designer to source a patterned valance fabric, and specified honeycomb cellular shades behind for thermal performance and light control. Installation took two days.

Contemporary living, Edina.

2023

The Specification

  • Motorized honeycomb cellular shades
  • Hardwired, integrated with home automation
  • Three rooms, twelve windows
  • Designer-led, dedicated trade account

[PLACEHOLDER COPY] A designer-led project for a recently renovated home. The client wanted minimalism — shades that disappeared into the architecture when raised, and operated silently. We specified motorized cellular shades with hardwired controls, integrated with the home’s existing Lutron system. The designer retained creative control; we handled the technical specification, sourcing, and installation.
